## Status

- Co-founder and coordinator of the [GeomScale](https://geomscale.github.io/) project, conducting R&D on high-dimensional geometric computing, statistics, and optimization. 

- Researcher at the [DSO lab](https://tto.ntua.gr/en/ntua-labs-en/school-rural-surveying-engineering-en/lab-dionysos-satellite-observatory-en/), NTUA, working on satellite trajectories, including methods and software for  precise orbit determination (POD) and positioning.

- Software engineer in the [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org/) project, focusing on Base development, the suite’s database front end and database management component.

- Lecturer at the [Department of Informatics and Telecommunications](https://www.di.uoa.gr/en/studies/graduate/cs) of [NKUA](https://en.uoa.gr/), teaching MSc courses and supervising diploma theses.

- Scientific collaborator with the [OURAGAN team](https://team.inria.fr/ouragan/) at INRIA.

## Short Bio

Spent a decade at Oracle/MySQL, developing geometric algorithms for GIS in [Boost.Geometry](https://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_64_0/libs/geometry/doc/html/index.html) and [MySQL](https://www.mysql.com/).

Previously, was a postdoctoral researcher in the [Algorithms Research Group](https://algo.ulb.be) at [Université libre de Bruxelles (ULB)](https://www.ulb.be/en), working with Prof. [Samuel Fiorini](https://samuel.fiorini.web.ulb.be) on combinatorial optimization and polyhedral combinatorics [cite:17][cite:20][cite:29]. Before that, was a visiting researcher at the National Institute for Mathematical Sciences in South Korea and a researcher at the University of Athens.

Holds a PhD in Computer Science from the [University of Athens](https://en.uoa.gr/), supervised by Prof. [Ioannis Emiris](https://cgi.di.uoa.gr/~emiris/index-eng.html), an MSc in Logic, Algorithms and Computation from the University of Athens, and a Diploma (5-year degree) in Computer Engineering & Computer Science from [CEID, University of Patras](https://www.ceid.upatras.gr/).

## Research Interests

- Foundations of data science: sampling, integration, optimization.
- Mathematical software, computational statistics, algorithm engineering, open-source software.
- Linear algebra, (high-dimensional) computational geometry, robust computation.
- GIS, spatial databases, satellite geodesy, precise orbit determination (POD), positioning.
- Applications in computational finance and structural biology.
